DUV International School Alumni Relations

The Office of Alumni Relations at DUV International School, in collaboration with various departments, units, and committed volunteers, supports alumni networks to further the school's mission and goals. Our alumni networks aim to connect former students who share similar interests, passions, and career paths, building a lifelong community that reflects the core values of DUV.

DUV Alumni Association

The DUV Alumni Association helps graduates stay connected to their school, peers, and teachers. The association hosts events, reunions, and networking opportunities that encourage lifelong friendships and professional development. Alumni can participate in school activities, offer mentorship to current students, and support school programs.
